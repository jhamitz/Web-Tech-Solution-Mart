What Does Digital Marketing Actually Mean?

Explaining in simple terms, the practice of using marketing strategies to promote a business only through the internet is known as Digital Marketing. Face-to-face interactions or traditional print platforms are now out of fashion as these methods can only help to reach a limited audience, but with online marketing, it’s possible to harness the power of World Wide Web (WWW) in order to reach a larger and potential audience.

Digital Marketing Methods

Different agencies use different marketing methods to promote different types of businesses. This is basically because different businesses require different types of audiences and considering this, we also use different digital marketing methods to improve your business growth.

Search Engine Optimization

It is basically a method of incorporating keywords into your web pages which ultimately helps your business to rank higher in search engine results. An intelligent SEO agency focuses on keywords that are both product-specific and location-specific. The ultimate goal is to predict words that potential customers would search for in any given search engine. Different search engines have a different programmed algorithm that acts as an index to quickly generate results.

  • Keywords and strategies incorporated to the main business website is categorized under on-page SEO.
  • Activities that take place outside the main website are categorized under off-page SEO, such as backlinks, domain authority and social media mentions.

Facebook Advertisements

Since it is the largest social media platform, there’s no doubt that advertising your business on Facebook is a great move. Currently, there are more than 2.5 billion active users on Facebook, and it keeps growing day by day. It is undoubtedly one of the easiest ways to get your business noticed through Facebook ads. Moreover, it’s possible to customize your audience for Facebook ads on the basis of age, income, location, etc.

Pay Per Click or PPC or Google Ads

Very similar to Facebook ads, Google Ads also drive inorganic traffic to your business page. Google holds the title for the world’s largest search engine, and it bridges effective SEO and paid ads suitably. The best part about PPC ads is that you (the client) can choose where to place the ad, i.e. sidebar, top of the page, mobile apps, news feed, etc.

Social Media Marketing

It is possibly the best way for creating brand awareness and establishing brand familiarity. For this, we have a team of experts who can create beautiful visual content for your audience. Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter, Pinterest, etc. are the most popular social media platforms today. Different platforms have their different stipulations for content creation, for example Instagram heavily relies on high-resolution images, but Twitter encourages sharing links and texts. Though, promotional images and videos should have a company logo to establish brand familiarity.

Content Marketing

It mainly focuses on creating original and in-depth information to make the users understand what your business goals are. In simple terms, content marketing is known as blogging, and it provides your audience valuable information about the services and products that your company offer. Keeping other things aside, we focus on creating the creative and easy to read content in order to attract a wider audience.

Email Marketing

Efficient e-mail marketing is essential for a digital marketing agency because today, people respond more to emails personalized with their name. It’s also an exclusive form of communication between customers and the company. We focus on creating suitable layouts, text formatting, and attention-grabbing headlines
